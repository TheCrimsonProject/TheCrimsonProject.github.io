Aesthetic
Behind the Project

Member languages of Crimson all follows one or more of the following ideas which work as the actual aesthetic goal.

Simplicity

The more the options, the harder the thorough understanding is to achive, and thus a language should be as simple as possible.

Capability

A language should be capable of handle every situation it was designed for.

Synaethesia

A language should also capable of doing other things if it's reasonable enough.

No Paradigm

Avoid all kinds of paradigm jargon, focus on actual language features.

Stoic

Do not pursue becoming a silver bullet.
